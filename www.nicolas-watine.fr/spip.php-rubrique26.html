• The GARDENS of SANS-SOUCIS
    www.nicolas-watine.fr

    The Gardens of Sans-Soucis of the Manor of Sarton are enlisted in
    - the Journées Européennes du Patrimoine
    - the Association Régionale des Parcs et Jardins du Nord / Pas-de -Calais
    - the Rendez-Vous aux Jardins
    - the Automnales aux Jardins





    The Gardens of Sans-Soucis are opened every days from June to September : 9.am to 19. pm. but closed every Monday and the 13th - 14th - 15th July 2012.

    They are opened the other days of the year by appointment : 
    03 - 21 -22 -09 -02

    They are located between Amiens and Arras at 8 km east of Doullens and 45 km from Péronne ( Historial de la Grande Guerre of 1914- 1918 ) .

    The entrance is : 
     - 5 euros for individuals
     - 4 euros for groups
     - free for children under 12 years

    The outside vist of the Manor as well as its Gardens can be free or guided.
    Gardens of classical inspiration where boxwood and yew topiary play in harmony with peartrees and appletrees on espaliers, on a short lawn cutted in English style.You could admire a beautiful collection of 400 old garden tools and a constant exhibition of Nicolas Watine’s paintings, owner of this place who created the Gardens in 1998 around a Manor of XVI- XVII th centu

    The COLLECTION of OLD GARDEN TOOLS

    This collection is composed of 400 old garden tools related to the cutting of boxwood and yews , to the treatment of plants , to the watering...
    You could discover a lot of secateurs , saws and compasses of gardener and so on.
